How Organizing Works

1

Connect

Tenants in a building connect, share concerns, and build relationships with neighbors facing similar housing issues.

2

Organize

Tenants meet regularly, identify shared demands (repairs, fair rent, protections), and form a tenant association.

3

Win

Organized tenants negotiate with landlords, win concrete improvements, and build lasting power in their buildings.
