# The French Strikes of May June 1968 Bruno Astarian

The French strikes of May-June 1968 - Bruno Astarian Date: Unknown Source: https://libcom.org/article/french-strikes-may-june-1968-bruno-astarian Tags: strikes, general strikes, France, unions, France 1968, CGT, CFDT, Bruno Astarian, PDF A demystifying review essay and analysis summarizing the events of May-June 1968 in France with an almost exclusive focus on the strikes of the workers, based on reports and testimonies garnered from a voluminous bibliography, providing a sobering reassessment of the largest nationwide strike in French history, which the author defines as a “generalized non-insurrectional work stoppage”.
