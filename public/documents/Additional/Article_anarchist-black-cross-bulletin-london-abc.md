# Anarchist Black Cross Bulletin London Abc

Anarchist Black Cross Bulletin - London ABC Date: Unknown Source: https://libcom.org/article/anarchist-black-cross-bulletin-london-abc Tags: 1990s, prisons, United Kingdom, publications, Anarchist Black Cross, PDF TheAnarchist Black Cross Bulletinwas produced by London ABC in the early 1990s, covering prisoner solidarity, prisons, miscarriages of justice etc. At least nine issues were published. The Bulletin was superceded byTaking Libertiesin the mid-90s. Issues below scanned in by the comrades at Sparrows Nest archive, Nottingham.
