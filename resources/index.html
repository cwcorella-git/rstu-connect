How to Use These Tools

1. Identify the Issue

Use the Habitability Checklist and Lease Review Checklist to identify what's wrong and whether it violates Nevada law.

2. Document Everything

Use the Repair Request Documentation Form to create a detailed record with photos, dates, and landlord responses.

3. Build Your Campaign

Use meeting templates and the Campaign Demand Template to organize your neighbors and form clear, achievable demands.

4. Get Legal Support

Contact Northern Nevada Legal Aid or the Self-Help Law Center for free legal assistance. Use our links to connect directly.
